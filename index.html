The User Group

dotnetsheff is a monthly user group focused on software development, particularly in the .NET ecosystem. We welcome people with interests in software development of all ages and levels of experience. If you or someone you know is interested in speaking, please get in touch via Twitter (@dotnetsheff) or email (organisers at dotnetsheff.co.uk).

Open to everyone

No matter if you are a student who wants to learn more or a senior developer wanting to share experiences, we welcome everyone along to the group to enjoy the event and network with like-minded technologists.

Always free

dotnetsheff is free and will always be free
